Guacamole recipe

Description

This mexican dish is well renowned throughout the world for its wonderful taste. It can be complemented with a variety of dishes especially tacos, ,oooooOOHH, dont that just makes your mouth water?

Recreating this dish is a piece of cake, you just need to throw in a bunch of ingredients and voila!, a magnificent dish was born on this holy planet. Lets get started shall we?

Ingredient List

  • 3 avacados -peeled,pitted and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Â½ cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (Optional)

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic
  2. Stir in cayenne pepper. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.
